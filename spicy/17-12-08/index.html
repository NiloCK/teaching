  5. A whole number has exactly $8$ positive divisors. If two of the divisors are $21$ and $35$, what is the number?

    Note: a number's divisors are numbers that evenly divide it. For example, the number $9$ has $3$ divisors: $1$, $3$, and $9$, since $9 \div 1 = 9$, $9 \div 3 = 3$, and $9 \div 9 = 1$. $2$ is not a divisor of $9$, because $9$ cannot be evenly split up into $2$ groups.

Module 5—Acids and Bases

Reflect and Connect

 

Discuss

 

Read the scoring criteria provided in the General Holistic Scoring Guide. Contact your teacher if you have any questions about the scoring guide.

 

Go to the Discussion board for your class and download a copy of the data and answers to the preceding Try This activity from one or more students. Read the data and the answers provided in the response.

 

On the document, enter comments that provide the following feedback to the author:

  • One positive comment (or more) identifying an aspect that has been done well. Use the criteria listed in the scoring guide to help you identify this aspect.

  • One change that could be made that would improve the quality of the response. Make reference to the criteria in the scoring guide to justify your suggestion.

Post the document containing your comments on the Discussion board, and save a copy to your course folder. Send a copy of your document to your teacher if instructed to do so.

 

Finally, review the comments from your peers of your own responses to the Try This activity. Revise your responses accordingly, and save a copy of your revised work in your course folder. Submit a second copy of your revised work to your teacher if instructed to do so.
